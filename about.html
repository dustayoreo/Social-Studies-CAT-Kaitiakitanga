About This Project

Our Purpose

This website was created as part of a Social Studies Project focused on environmental issues in New Zealand, mainly focusing on recycling contamination, product waste, and what role the National Government played in relation to all of this.

What We Investigated

We researched how recycling systems worked in New Zealand, what causes contamination, and how government policies, businesses, and consumers all contribue to New Zealand's environmental issues.
