New Zealand's Environmental Issues

Recycling Contamination

One of the major causes of recycling contamination is people placing non-recyclable rubbish into recycling bins or not cleaning recyclable items before disposal. Food residues, liquids, and mixed substances can contaminate entire loads of recycling, making it either extremely difficult or impossible to process.
Government websites mainly focus on the benefits of recycling, although they also acknowledge challenges such as contamination and difficulties in processing some materials.
