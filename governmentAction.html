Government Action

The New Zealand Government has introduced severl policies to reduce waste, improve recycling systems, and encourage more sustainable practices. However, it is heavily questioned if these solutions will go far enough and if it will be effective, as well as the fact that the Government is going as far to undoing the mining for fossil fuels ban - creating a conflict of interest.

Waste Strategy

Thee Government has released a new Waste Strategy and Waste Work Programme to reduce landfill waste and improve recycling.

Managing Contamination

Councils continue to encouage and spread awareness about cleaning recyclable materials and preventing contamination.

Current Debates

Stronger solutions and alternatives are needed, while the Government has stated that working with businesses and communities will improve the waste management over time.
